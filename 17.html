    Dorothy Francis


    Dorothy was the eldest of the 5 children.

    Born on 2nd March 1898
    Married: James Shaunessy Elliott
    In 1920
    At St Patrick's Church Blacktown

    They started their family at Quakers Hill then moved to Rooty Hill, Wentworthville, Westmead and then finally settled in Harris Park with the 11 children.

    Dorothy attended the Scott's Commercial College at Parramatta where she learnt book keeping then went to work in the office of her father's business.

    Dorothy was excellent on the piano and could sing as well.

    Dorothy installed in her children a deep appreciation of their Catholic heritage and helped them to achieve a level of deep faith which w as to greatly assist in their trials in later life.

    She brought her children up to he ladies and gentlemen, hard workers, bright and they were always ready to chip in and lend a hand. She also trained them well to be multiskilled around the house (when they weren't trying to escape from their chores).

    It was a happy family centred around their mother particularly on Sunday nights around thepiano! The memory pf those afternoons/evenings linger happy in the memories of all.

    Died 24 November 1972 (age 74) and buried with James in section 14 of Rookwood Catholic Cemetery.
