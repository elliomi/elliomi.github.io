    Vince was born at Wentworthville on 16/12/1938 and moved to 85 Alfred Street Granville on approximately 26/1/1940.

    Vince started school in 1944 at Blessed Oliver Plunkett School at Harris Park until completing 3rd class, then went to Marist Bros Parramatta until he completed 6th class.

    Because he wanted to be a tradesman when he left school, Vince then attended Granville Secondary Technical School to gain his intermediate certificate that he did in 1954.

    During Vince's last few years at school, he worked in a grocers shop in the afternoons and Saturday mornings.

    In February 1955. Vince commenced my apprenticeship as a patternmaker at Regents Park and attended Sydney Technical College for 4 years.

    Vince and Jenny met at a CYO meeting at Harris Park in March 1956. Jenny was Vince's only girlfriend and they were engaged in December 1960.

    During Vince's earlier years he became very involved in basketball including playing coaching and administration. This continued until he dislocated his shoulder in October 1959.

    After Vince completed his apprenticeship, he changed jobs and worked with a large foundry in Alexandria for 7 years, before going to another foundry that offered him a charge hands position. Yin stayed with that Company for another 7 years filling the positions of foreman and finally assistant manager. Yin finally left that company in 1974 to go into a business with 3 other people. This business, 1 finally closed in June 2000 for health reasons.

    Vince have since been working for a company in Cessnock designing, building and commissioning machinery to do the same sort of work that was done in his own business prior to closure.

    After getting married in 1962, Vince and Jenny lived with Jenny's parents in a self contained flat for 20 months until they moved into their own new home at Woodpark (Merrylands West) where they lived until April 1989 when they moved to our current address at Baulkham Hills.
