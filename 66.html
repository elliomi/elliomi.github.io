    James Ignatius Maguire (1932 to 1996)

    James Maguire had one sister and 3 brothers, Moira, Ray, Colin and Kevin. They lived off Woodville Road behind the Granville Technical College. Parents, Rose 24.3.01 to 25.4.1973 and Joseph.

    Prior to meeting the Elliott family James Maguire went to a school, which prepared students for priesthood. He came out of the seminary prior to completion and ordination. Ray Maguire played tennis at Granville with Len Elliott. Jim also started to play tennis and that is when he and Len became good mates. After the seminary Jim worked for Jacks Tyre Company in Merrylands and then applied to work in the Public Service. Jim worked for many years in the office of the "Master of Lunacy" as it was then called. They were the Trustees of the finances of those institutionalized. Jim stayed with the public service until his early retirement.

    Len would meet Jim after work in the city and they would go to the pub for a drink and also play tennis together and "knock around" as dad put it. Jim Maguire met Doreen through his friendship with Len.
