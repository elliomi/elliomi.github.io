Lenoard John Elliott
  • Born: 26 Jun 1932, Wentworthville
  • Married 16 Nov 1957, Blessed Oliver Plunkett Harris Park, to Shirley Rose Elliott (Osborne)
  • Occupation: Electrician
    Len comes from a big family with a total of 11 brothers and sisters. Len is number 8.

    Leonard John Elliott was born to Dorothy Francis (Wright) and James Shaunessy Elliott on 26 June 1932 at Fulligah Road, Wentworthville. (Opposite the public school).

    Went to Primary school at St Columbus Catholic school at Wentworthville, across the paddock behind their home till 4th class.

    Joined his Brothers at Marist Brothers Parramatta and eventually persuaded his mum to let him go to Granville Technical School which was more focused on trades rather than languages.

    James Elliott died when dad was doing his intermediate certificate and hence, instead of taking up a teaching scholarship he went to work and learnt his trade as electrician. Len did his apprenticeship with Stowe Electric then located in the city.

    Len met Shirley at a dance and they went out for about 5 years before eventually marrying. They were married at Blessed Oliver Plunkett Church, Harris Park (as it was then called). They spent their honeymoon at Berowra Waters.
