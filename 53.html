    As noted, Allan was the first of the Elliott boys to meet the girls at Lewisham hospital. Allan was dating Mavis and hence the introductions of Lawrence and Marge and Albert and Val.

    He went to war with his brother Albert and they both served in Papua New Guinea. Allan was a transport driver in the Army and after the war was mainly a bus driver at Parramatta. Allan, like Albert, would also answer the Mass in Latin.

    The family originally lived on Victoria Road, Parramatta Road then settled at Telopea. Allen and Mavis retired to Ballina in later years.
